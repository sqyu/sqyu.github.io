Description

Native country. Born and raised in Shenzhen (深圳) in Guangdong (廣東) (a.k.a. Canton Province).

Of the 31 provinces (省直辖市自治区) of Mainland China, I have been to 12, all in the south except for Beijing:

  • Guangdong (广东)

  • Sichuan (四川)

  • Beijing (北京)

  • Chongqing (重庆)

  • Fujian (福建)

  • Guangxi (广西)

  • Guizhou (贵州)

  • Hubei (湖北)

  • Hunan (湖南)

  • Jiangsu (江苏)

  • Shanghai (上海)

  • Zhejiang (浙江)
